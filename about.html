About CB Electric

A Legacy of Excellence in Electrical Services

Babb Electric was founded by Charlie Babb, a proud graduate of Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. With roots in Brawley, California, and Mission Bay, San Diego, our company has grown to serve a wide array of clients and industries. Starting with small household projects and expanding to large-scale industrial facilities, our mission has always been to provide reliable, high-quality electrical services.

Our team has experience in a variety of industries, including:

  • Industrial agriculture facilities such as produce coolers and hay presses
  • Marine electrical systems
  • Everyday household electrical needs

At Babb Electric, we are proud to combine technical expertise with a customer-focused approach, ensuring that every project is completed to the highest standards. Whether it's a residential repair, a commercial installation, or an industrial upgrade, our team is here to power your future.
