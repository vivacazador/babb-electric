About Us

Learn more about our history, mission, and the services we proudly offer.

CB Electric was founded by Charlie Babb, a proud graduate of Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. With roots in Brawley, California, and Mission Bay, San Diego, our company has grown to serve a wide array of clients and industries. Starting with small household projects and expanding to large-scale industrial facilities, our mission has always been to provide reliable, high-quality electrical services.

  • Industrial Agriculture: Supporting produce coolers and hay presses with expert electrical solutions.
  • Marine Electrical Systems: Ensuring reliability for marine vessels and systems.
  • Residential Services: Handling everything from small repairs to major upgrades in homes.
